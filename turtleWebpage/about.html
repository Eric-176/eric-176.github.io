About Us

We love turtles here at Gunnersbury Catholic School and hope to one day help the turtles reclaim the earth as our rightful rulers. For too long have they been oppressed under an anti turtle regime and this injustice has occurred under our very eyes. No longer I tell you my brothers shall we bow to these low lifes who steal our lives, our hopes and our very futures. Raise your flippers my comrade and fight something much greater than you'll ever come to know. For it is only due to adversity that we have become complacent and through adversity that we shall reclaim our honour.
